About us

Welcome to Arcadia, where we want to make your special occasions elegant and beautiful! Arcadia is devoted to providing flowers and floral decor to any event; from graduations to weddings. We provide a gorgeous selection of products whether you want one bouquet or arrangements for large events.

Feel free to get in touch with us or even book a consultation for event inquiries.

Fast Delivery

Our company has guaranteed 3 to 5 day delivery and same day pick up for local orders.

Top Quality

We spare no expsense with our flowers! Arcadia uses the highest grade of both flowers and arrangment accessories.

Customization

We believe bouquets are personal. If you're having trouble finding the perfect bouquet, we provide a customization service where you could build your own with the right flowers!
