Frequently Asked Questions

Still got questions?
How long does it take to deliver my orders?

We guarantee 3 to 5 day delivery and same day pick up for local orders. Place your orders by 12pm for same day pick up.

How would Coronavirus affect my orders?

Orders may take longer than usual to be delivered as our team is trying their best to maintain a sanitized work environment.

Can I request for specific flowers or designs?

Absolutely! We believe bouquets are personal. If you're having trouble finding the perfect bouquet, we provide a customization service where you could build your own with the right flowers!

How do I track my orders?

To track your orders, we kindly ask you to sign in to your account first. If you are already signed in, you can track your order here.
